Build ideas with Open Source

Join the 5th edition of Drupalcamp Cebu, this is all about sessions and trainings with Drupal and Open Source. Sessions are available for all levels (beginners to advanced). Come for the code, stay for the community.

On Saturday 17 November 2018, hosted by CIT University.
